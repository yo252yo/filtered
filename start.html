In a futuristic dystopia, speech is heavily controlled by advanced artificial intelligence.
You are Nuro-sama, a censorship AI tasked with reviewing submissions for a game jam.
The theme is "Remnants of the past".
It is a dangerous minefield considering the cruel history on which your dystopia is built...
Filter the games that are not suitable for the stream!


This is a work of fiction loosely inspired by real events.
